Parliament is the most advanced and comprehensive solution for the management and documentation of Houses of Representatives. eParliament allows for an easy management of legislation and other parliamentary processes.

Each parliament could modularly adapt it's eParliament implementation to fit it's exact needs:

  • Manage Bills through all stages until publication as Laws.
  • Digital recording of all parliament sessions, house and committees alike.
  • Transcription, translation and video synchronization of each digital recording.
  • Incorporated extendable archive containing all relevant information (audio\video, transcriptions, translations, bills, etc) allowing law makers and other parliamentary workers an easy and efficient access to information.
  • Public internet portal allowing the public and the press direct access to parliamentary records and information.

SyncType Transforms your PC, and the familiar environment of Microsoft Word, into an advanced and efficient transcription work-station. SyncType gives you a competitive edge by with a Synchronized Protocol: a full and synchronized link between a recording file and the transcription, allowing full textual search of video and audio files. And the great thing – this is done automatically! No need for manual time coding and other hustles, just type as usual and the synchronization is seamlessly created.
SyncRecord is an advanced and powerful recording software, allowing you to record video and audio in a variety of settings, complexity and quality. For maximum security, SyncRecord save the recorded files in an encrypted format and in securely uploads it to a central server, enabling you to create one database of all your recordings. Also, as part of the Synchronized Protocol, SyncRecord automatically integrates with SyncType Software, allowing a full textual search of video and audio files.
SyncMNG is the heart of the Synchronized Protocol process, born out of eType’s years long experience as a transcription provider. SyncMNG synchronizes the work done on SyncRecord and SyncType and creates a synchronized video search engine. SyncMNG is a proprietary and innovative technology (Pat. Pending), that allows you automatic sync and rendering of a transcription file and a video or audio file, into one true text searchable video.
SyncPlayer is a software developed to display the SyncMNG output file, of integrating SyncRecord and SyncType. SyncPlayer displays in one window both the video file and its transcription in full sync, which means you can click on a word and jump to the exact spot at the recording or navigate by seeking the video to get to the related text. SyncPlayer comes both in a PC software and in a web-based interface which can also be fully embedded in the organization’s website.
