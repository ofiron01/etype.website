Law is an advance comprehensive digital solution for Law, investigative and regulatory organizations, already in use by the leading Israeli investigating agencies.

Leave in your past the process of manually searching through huge piles of pages and folders, of hours of seeking through video tapes. eLaw is a modular solution covering all aspects of investigation management:

  • Design and equip digital investigation rooms.
  • Digital Recording of all sessions.
  • Easley create transcription and translation of your investigation recordings, while keeping synchronization of your digital recordings.
  • Get an incorporated archive of all your textual files and audio\video recordings. Click a word in the text and jump directly to that moment in the video, or seek through the video to find that moment in the text.
  • Search through your full investigative file, complete with all your digital recordings.
  • Automatically find connections between related investigations with automatic semantic processing of the files.
  • Mark an important segment while recording.
  • Give real-time instruction whether to transcribe a specific segment.
  • Mark an important segment while recording.
