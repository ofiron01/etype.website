ABOUT US

Type develops and implements solutions for digital recordings and the production of protocols, with audio, video & text synchronization.

These innovative solutions are unique to eType and allow for better transparency and use of textual protocols. eType specializes in developing advanced technologies for recording, transcription, translation and documentation and is based on many years of experience in providing these services to the highest quality.
All services provided by eType are based on the technological tools developed in house, and sold around the world. The constant improvement of these tools and services allows us to give our costumers the best possible service, turnaround times, in competetive pricing.

our clients

Type is a subsidiary of Omnitech Ltd, the company aims to provide technological products and services to create the ultimate transctiption tools for the Israeli and the international market.

Towards this end eType has produced a series of innovative software tools for synchronized transcription, translation and ‘transcription workflow management’. eType is the leading transcription services provider in Israel: from the Knesset (the Israeli parliament), through courts, arbitration tribunals, security agencies, media production, private investigators, managements, boards of directors, international conferences, television and more.
